Is it possible to identify the stages of argentine rock by the topics its lyrics has along time? In this work we analyzed the relation between lyrics and history by natural language processing techniques.
<!--more-->

We scraped song lyrics from the webpage [https://rock.com.ar/](https://rock.com.ar/) and worked with a corpus of 23.258 lyrics from the sixties to contemporary music. We applied a Latent Dirichlet Allocation model to detect relevant topics and analyzed the evolution of those over time. 

This work was presented in the [XIX Sociology Conference](http://jornadasdesociologia2021.sociales.uba.ar/altaponencia/?acciones2=ver&id_mesa=186&id_ponencia=1179) of the University of Buenos Aires.
