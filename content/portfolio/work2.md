Web application that calculates the cost of a year of menstrual cycle: based on quantities of periods, quantity of tampons or pads used per period and brands.
<!--more-->

I developed this [webapp](https://layitx.shinyapps.io/menstruaccion_app/) through ShinyApps, as part of **EcoFeminita** for the [**#Menstruacción**](https://ecofeminita.com/menstruaccion/) campagin, which proposes three projects:

- the removal of value added tax to menstrual health products
- free disposal of those products in communitary spaces, such as schools and hospitals
- the development of investigations and divulgation of data regarding menstrual health.
