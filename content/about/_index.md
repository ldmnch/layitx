Hi, I'm Laia.

I'm 23 years old and currently finishing my Sociology Licenciate degree at the University of Buenos Aires.

I'm a research assistant at [factor~data (University of San Martín)](www.twitter.com/factorData), where we combine social and computational sciences in through projects. In UNSAM I also teach CSS at different levels: 
- I'm a teaching assistant in **Data processing and statistics with R for Social Sciences** at the Sociology Licenciate degree.
- I am a lecturer in **Data visualization** at the postgraduate specialization on Computational Social Science. 

Also, I am the coordinator of [Ecofeminita's](https://ecofeminita.com/ecofemidata/) data section, where we visualise gender inequality through the dissemination of data, statistics, academic content and original production aimed at all audiences.
